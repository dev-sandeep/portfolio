This Privacy Policy describes how extensions, applications, and related software (collectively, the “Apps”) created by the developer identified below handle information when you use them.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all Apps published by the same creator, including browser extensions and other software products made available by:

  • Developer contact: uplinksandy9@gmail.com

Unless an individual App states otherwise, the practices described here apply to every App published under this developer account.

2. Summary

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.
  • We do not operate backend servers that collect or store your personal data.
  • Any information needed for an App to work is stored locally on your device only.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, receive, or process personal information on our servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Account credentials
  • Payment or billing information
  • Precise location data
  • Health, financial, or other sensitive categories of personal data
  • Browsing history sent to us or stored on our servers

4. Local Device Storage

Some Apps may save preferences and functional data only on your device (for example, using browser storage APIs such as chrome.storage.local). This may include items such as:

  • App settings and preferences
  • Timer, session, or usage statistics stored locally for your convenience
  • Lists you configure within the App (for example, blocked or allowed sites)
  • Backup or export files you choose to save or import yourself

This data stays on your device unless you choose to export, copy, or share it. We do not automatically upload this data to our servers.

5. How Apps Use Permissions

Some Apps may request browser or device permissions to provide their core features (for example: storage, notifications, alarms, tab access, or site blocking). These permissions are used only to perform functions you expect from the App and are not used to collect personal information for us.

Processing related to these permissions happens locally on your device.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may disclose information only if required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request, and only to the extent legally required.

7. Third-Party Services

Our Apps are designed to operate without transmitting your data to third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services controlled by us.

If you download an App through a platform (such as the Chrome Web Store), that platform may collect information according to its own privacy policy. We do not control platform policies or practices.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Because data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data remains until you remove it, reset the App, or uninstall the App
  • Uninstalling an App generally removes locally stored App data from your browser or device
  • You may be able to export or delete data using in-App settings where available

9. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Users

Because we do not collect personal information on our servers, cross-border data transfers by us generally do not occur. Local data remains on your device under your control.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding personal data. Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, most requests can be fulfilled by managing or deleting data stored locally on your device, or by uninstalling the App.
